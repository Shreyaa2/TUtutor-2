The Idea Behind TUTutor

After years of college, it has been observed that there is a LACKING of available tutors on campus. The department tutors
were becoming overwhelmed at the traffic of students needing help, and not being able to help everyone. Students have
become forced to search for outside help, but that can be time consuming, costly, and at times, unsafe.

Posts of tutoring concerns have been expressed online (see below):

Something needed to change...so that is what we did!

Introducing TUTutor! The convenient and safe platform for tutors and pupils to connect
so that the tutors can teach others in courses they excel in, and pupils can get the help that they deserve!
This platform is openly only for verified Towson University students and faculty, which ensures that trusted
connections are being made. This website is growing and becoming better each day, and we hope you enjoy it!

-TUTutor Admin
